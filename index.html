Our Process

How We Work

Every project we do is personalized. We design according to your requirements, helping you choose fragrance, colors and more — combining sizes and shapes to create a unique atmosphere.

01

Initial Consultation

We meet to get to know each other and understand your project. You tell us about the garden of your dreams and we listen carefully to every detail.

02

Site Visit or Remote

If you are in Mallorca, we organise a visit to the place. If you are elsewhere, you send us all the information we need to begin developing the preliminary project.

03

3D Design & Planning

The outcome is a project with stunning 3D renderings and technical plans where you can visualise the finished project in detail before execution begins.

04

Execution

Once you have approved the project, we begin to execute that dream you have always wanted. We oversee every step to ensure the result matches your vision.

How can I help you?

We can meet you so you can tell us about the project.

If you are in Mallorca, we will organize a visit to the place and take your ideas to make real your dream. Or maybe, if you are in another place, you will send us all the information we request in order to begin developing the preliminary project.
