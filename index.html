Our Process

How We Work

Every project we do is personalized! We design according to your requirements. We will help you choose fragrance, colors and more, combining sizes and shapes in order to create a unique atmosphere.

01

Initial Consultation

We will have a meeting to get to know the place and we will give you the opportunity to tell us what the garden of your dreams looks like.

02

Site Visit or Remote

If you are in Mallorca, we will organize a visit to the place and take your ideas to make real your dream. Or maybe, if you are in another place, you will send us all the information we request in order to begin developing the preliminary project.

03

3D Design & Planning

The outcome will be a project with 3D images of the garden through plans where you can see the finished project.

04

Execution

Once you accepted, we will begin to execute that dream that you want so much.

How can I help you?

Have an idea but don't know where to start? Whether it's a garden, a terrace, or a full interior renovation — let's talk. Every great project begins with a conversation.
